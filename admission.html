Physical Reporting Instructions for the candidates who have been allotted our college through Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Counseling – 2023 and have Frozen their choices as one of the Courses / Branches in AKGEC during UPTAC Counselling – 2023

Reporting is the process of completion of admission formalities by submitting requisite fees and documents at College in accordance with the terms and conditions stated in the Allotment Letter of UPTAC 2023 and as specified hereunder:
1) During the physical reporting at the college, candidates are required to fill out the admission form provided by the institute. Additionally, candidates must download the specified documents, complete them by hand, and personally submit the documents to the college authorities along with the admission form. The Candidates are required to bring the following original documents for verification, along with their photocopies, in the specified order when physically reporting at the college for admission.


List of documents :

  1. Application Form for Student ID Card
  2. Medical Certificate
  3. Transport Form(If required)
  4. Anti-Raggin Affidavits(Self and Parents). It is mandatory for all students to submit the Anti -raggin form online through the web portal .
